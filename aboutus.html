About Osborne Construction


Osborne Construction & Excavating was founded with the goal of giving customers in the Kitchener-Waterloo and surrounding areas high quality excavating services. We are constantly participating in new training to improve our skills and techniques to best serve our customers. From the moment you call Osborne Construction & Excavating, you will find we go the extra mile to make you a very satisfied customer. Mark Osborne been involved in the construction and excavating industry for more than a decade, working with both residential and commercial properties. He is trained and certified in many areas of home improvements, including renovations and insurance claims. Mark Osborne loves the opportunities to meet new people and help them find the best solutions for their home and or commercial needs. He strives to provide high-quality services and uses the best equipment of Bobcat brands to always meet customer expectations.
